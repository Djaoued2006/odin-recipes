Making baghrir

this is an image of the bagrir recipe

this traditional recipe has its origin from Algeria , the country of the best food in the world , it is a recipe that all people in algeria agree that it's the best especially when they take it with their coffee!

all algerians (me also) know that there isn't a recipe better than el baghrir , especially at the afternoon , after a long day with some amount of honey!!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 5 eggs
  • milk
  • 2 sugar
  • much honey
  • much love

Steps

  1. we do the flour in the plate
  2. we do the eggs on the flour
  3. we add the milk to the plate we're working on
  4. we merge all the things well
  5. we add sugar to it (one or one and a half of a cup of sugar only)
  6. we add our love to it and we let it tekhmer
  7. after letting it tekhmer for some 15 minutes , we cook it in the makla and we add honey
  8. that's it , add love to it (it's my secret)
